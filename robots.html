  • What is robots.txt and how have you configured it for your site?

  • robots.txt is for programs that travel across the web, visiting different websites. The first thing they will do when they come to a new website is look for the robots.txt file and take action according to what the file contains. Programs with malicious intent will just ignore the file however.
    I set my robots.txt to apply for all programs and told them not to visit any of my sites.
